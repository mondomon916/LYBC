About

LeaveYourBusinessCard is an online resource which creates an easy and convenient method to promote and market any business of any size. Unlike similar sites, we do not require you to become a member in order to view listings of services you seek. If you choose to become a member, you will be able to take advantage of a marketing focused social network for your business. Anyone can join, anyone can become a member, and it’s a quick and easy enrollment process.

A little history of how LeaveYourBusinessCard came to be … My fiancé and her friend had recently started an event planning company. She wanted me to pass out a few of her cards to help promote her business services. Long story short, I was the typical lazy guy and never got around to it. As I was thinking of how lazy and a “not-so-good” of a fiancé that I was being, I began coming up with ideas of how I could do something, but not really having to do much at the same time.

Then the idea hit me, “what if I created a virtual place that everyone and anyone could visit and either leave a business card or view a business card from someone that had services they were looking for?” This idea allowed me to save some face and at the same time not having to do much at all! Thus began the idea, the development, the website LeaveYourBusinessCard.com.
